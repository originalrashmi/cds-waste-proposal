01

The Problem We Are Solving

The same 12 data fields — entered three times, by three different people, on three different forms.

1

Handwritten GMP Bag Label

Operator writes drug name, batch, quantity, strength, and form by hand at the collection bench.

× No timestamp recorded
× No operator identity captured
× Legibility risk on handwritten text
2

Paper Form 0010 at Drum

Same data manually re-copied from the GMP label onto Form 0010 at the vault drum — by hand.

× Human transcription errors introduced
× No system validation on entry
× Paper can be lost, damaged, or misfiled
3

Manual Spreadsheet Entry

Same data typed into a spreadsheet by a different person, at a later time, from the paper forms.

× Not contemporaneous — ALCOA+ risk
× No link between entry and physical drum
× Discrepancies require rework
This is not a people problem. It is a process problem. And it has a straightforward fix.

Principle Current State Risk Level
Attributable

Contemporaneous
Was it recorded at the time?		 Spreadsheet entry happens hours or days after the event. HIGH
Original
Is this the first record?		 The spreadsheet is a copy of a copy of a handwritten note. HIGH
Accurate
Is the data correct?		 Transcription from handwriting to Form 0010 to spreadsheet introduces error at each step. HIGH
Enduring
Will it last?		 Paper stored in vault. Subject to physical damage or loss. MEDIUM
An FDA inspector or DEA auditor reviewing ALCOA+ compliance would identify multiple gaps. Phase 1 closes all of them.
03

Enter Once. Print. Done.

Phase 1 replaces the handwritten GMP label and the paper Form 0010 with a single digital entry at the point of collection.

1

Operator opens form on Surface Pro tablet

Microsoft Forms via Edge browser, pinned as the default screen. No login steps during the shift.

Microsoft Forms
2

Select dropdowns for all controlled fields

Substance, schedule, drum, area, dosage form via dropdowns. Only batch number and quantity are typed. Dropdown lists controlled by DEA Compliance.

Controlled Dropdown Lists
3

Operator name + timestamp captured automatically

Microsoft 365 identity — the logged-in Windows account provides name and email. Tamper-proof. No typing required.

M365 Identity — Auto-Capture
4

Submit — data flows instantly to SharePoint

Entry appears in the SharePoint workbook in real time. DEA Compliance can see every entry from their desk immediately.

Power Automate + SharePoint
5

Label prints automatically at collection station

BarTender Enterprise generates a label with all fields, a Code 128 barcode of the drum number, and a QR code linking to the full digital record. Operator applies it to the bag.

BarTender Enterprise + Honeywell PM43
