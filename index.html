Phase 1 Process Flow

How the new digital workflow replaces three manual steps with a single entry point.

New Digital Process

1

Open Form on Tablet

Operator picks up Surface Pro at collection bench. Form is already open — no login needed during shift.

Microsoft Forms via Edge
2

Select Dropdowns + Enter Batch & Qty

Drug, schedule, form, strength, drum, area via dropdowns. Only batch number and quantity are typed.

Controlled dropdown lists
3

Submit — Data Flows Automatically

Operator ID + timestamp captured by M365. Data written to SharePoint. Label sent to PM43 printer.

Power Automate + BarTender
4

Apply Printed Label to Bag

Label prints automatically with all fields, barcode, and QR code. No handwriting required.

Honeywell PM43
5

Bag Goes to Drum — Done

DEA Compliance sees the entry in real time. No form to fill. No spreadsheet to update. No reconciliation.

Old Paper Process (Eliminated)

1

Handwrite GMP Bag Label

Operator writes 12 fields by hand. Legibility issues. No timestamp. No identity capture.

Eliminated
2

Carry Bag to Vault + Find Form 0010

Operator locates the correct paper form physically attached to the drum in the vault.

Eliminated
3

Re-copy All 12 Fields onto Form 0010

Same data transcribed again by hand. Transcription errors introduced. Second touch point.

Eliminated
4

Collect Forms + Re-key into Spreadsheet

Different person, different time. Third entry of the same data. ALCOA+ violation — not contemporaneous.

Eliminated
5

Reconcile Discrepancies

DEA Compliance compares handwritten labels vs. forms vs. spreadsheet. Rework delays drum pickup.

Eliminated
